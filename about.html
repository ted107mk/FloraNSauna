About Us

Flora & Sauna is a not-for-profit corporation positioning itself to become the leading service for transactions of holistic medicinal to a collective that might be prevented from doing so otherwise due to ailing health or financial inability. This corporation utilizes a patient-focused approach to collective services that minimizes cost and inconvenience for it’s members. This corporation also hopes to set the standard as the most environmentally-friendly collective service in the bay area. In a time where collective services are interested more with their own product than their patients’, it’s important that there be an organization dedicated to it’s members holistic needs, to remain honest and accountable for ourselves, each other and the world that we share.
